# Research

[Back to README](./README.md) | [Projects](./projects.md) | [Experience](./experience.md)

My research focuses on AI-assisted STEM education, robotics learning, adaptive feedback systems, LLM-driven tutoring, simulation-based learning, and multimodal AI.

I am especially interested in intelligent systems that support learning, decision-making, and human-centered AI applications.

## Current Research Direction

### AI-Assisted Robotics Education

I work on systems that help students learn programming and robotics through structured content, browser-based coding, simulation, automated unit testing, and LLM-assisted feedback.

Related case study: [AI-Assisted Robotics Learning Platform](./docs/ai-assisted-robotics-platform.md)

### LLM-Based Tutoring and Feedback

My work explores how large language models can support learners with contextual feedback, code guidance, personalized explanations, and learning analytics.

### Multimodal AI

I build and evaluate pipelines that combine image evidence, OCR text, and textual context for practical AI applications.

Related case study: [Hate and Threat Detection in Digital Forensics](./docs/multimodal-forensics-ai.md)
